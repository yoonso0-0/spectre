template<size_t VolumeDim, typename ObservationValueTag, typename... Tensors, typename EventRegistrars>
class dg::Events::ObserveVolumeIntegrals< VolumeDim, ObservationValueTag, tmpl::list< Tensors... >, EventRegistrars >

Observe the volume integrals of the tensors over the domain.

Writes reduction quantities:

  • ObservationValueTag
  • Volume = volume of the domain
  • VolumeIntegral(*) = volume integral of the tensor
