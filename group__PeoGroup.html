◆ VARIABLE_CAUSES_CLANG_FPE

#define VARIABLE_CAUSES_CLANG_FPE (   var)    ((void)(var))

Clang's optimizer has a known bug that sometimes produces spurious FPEs. This indicates that the variable var can trigger that bug and prevents some optimizations. (See the LLVM bug report, which is effectively WONTFIX.)
