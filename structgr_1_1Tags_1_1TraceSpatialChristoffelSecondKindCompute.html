template<size_t SpatialDim, typename Frame, typename DataType>

Compute item for the trace of the spatial Christoffel symbols of the second kind \(\Gamma^{i} = \Gamma^{i}_{jk}\gamma^{jk}\) computed from the Christoffel symbols of the second kind and the inverse spatial metric.
