33 /// Create a 2D Domain consisting of four rotated Blocks.
34 /// - The lower left block has its logical \f$\xi\f$-axis aligned with
35 /// the grid x-axis.
36 ///
37 /// - The lower right block is rotated a half-turn (180 degrees) relative to the
38 /// lower left block.
39 ///
40 /// - The upper left block is rotated a quarter-turn counterclockwise
41 /// (+90 degrees) relative to the lower left block.
42 //
43 /// - The upper right block is rotated a quarter-turn clockwise
44 /// (-90 degrees) relative to the lower left block.
45 ///
46 /// This DomainCreator is useful for testing code that deals with
47 /// unaligned blocks.

57  CoordinateMaps::ProductOf2Maps<CoordinateMaps::Affine,
58  CoordinateMaps::Affine>>>;
