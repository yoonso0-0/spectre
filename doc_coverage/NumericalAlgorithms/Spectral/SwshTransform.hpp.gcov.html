     336             :  *   \f$m\f$ modes must be inferred from conjugation and further sign changes.

     338             :  *   quantities. Therefore, additional steps are taken (involving further
     339             :  *   conjugation of the provided data) in order to use those utilities on

     381             :  * \f}
