template<typename Lhs>
struct Cce::PreSwshDerivatives<::Tags::Multiplies< Lhs, Tags::BondiJbar > >

Compute the product of \(\bar{J}\) and the quantity represented by Rhs.

Details

In this function, \(\bar{J}\) is obtained via conjugation of Tags::BondiJ inline, rather than accessing Tags::BondiJbar in storage.
