Detailed Description

template<typename ArgumentTag>
struct Cce::ApplySwshJacobianInplace< Spectral::Swsh::Tags::Derivative< ArgumentTag, Spectral::Swsh::Tags::EthEthbar > >

Specialization for the spin-weighted derivative \(\eth \bar{\eth}\).

Details

The implemented equation is:

\[ \eth \bar{\eth} F = \eth^\prime \bar{\eth}^\prime F - \frac{\eth R \bar{\eth} R}{R^2} (1 - y)^2 \partial_y^2 F - (1 - y)\left(\frac{\eth R}{R} \bar{\eth} \partial_y F + \frac{\bar{\eth} R}{R} \eth \partial_y F + \frac{\eth \bar\eth R}{R} \partial_y F\right), \]

where \(\eth \bar{\eth}\) is the derivative at constant Bondi radius \(r\) and \(\eth^\prime \bar{\eth}^\prime\) is the derivative at constant numerical radius \(y\).
