struct  NormalOneForm
 NormalOneForm(i) is \(s_i\), the (unnormalized) normal one-form to the surface, expressed in Cartesian components. This is computed by \(x_i/r-\partial r_{\rm surf}/\partial x^i\), where \(x_i/r\) is Rhat and \(\partial r_{\rm surf}/\partial x^i\) is DxRadius. See Eq. (8) of [7]. Note on the word "normal": \(s_i\) points in the correct direction (it is "normal" to the surface), but it does not have unit length (it is not "normalized"; normalization requires a metric).
